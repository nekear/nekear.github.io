About BAND
BAND is a rock band formed in 2012. The band consists of four members: vocalist/guitarist Bill, bassist Ted, drummer Bob and guitarist/keyboardist Kevin. The band's debut album, "Sound of Music", was released in 2014. Their second album, "More Sound of Music", was released in 2016. The band is currently working on their third album, "Eclipse of Sound", which is slated for release in 2018.
